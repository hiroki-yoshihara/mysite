Famous Spot

Tokyo Tower

Tokyo Tower is located in Minato Ward. Tokyo Tower is 333m high and was built in 1958. The ticket price is 1200 yen at the main deck and 3000 yen at the top deck tour.

Tokyo Sky Tree

Tokyo Sky Tree is located in Sumida Ward. Tokyo Sky Tree is 634m high. Tokyo Sky Tree was built as a radio tower in May 2012.
